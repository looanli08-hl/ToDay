## Description

Your day becomes a beautiful scroll — automatically.

Unfold passively records where you go and what you do throughout the day. No manual input needed. Just carry your phone and let your daily story write itself.

**A Beautiful Daily Timeline**
Open Unfold at night and see your entire day laid out as an elegant vertical scroll — from dawn to dusk, with warm gradients that shift with the time of day. Every place you visited, every walk you took, every commute — all automatically captured and beautifully presented.

**AI That Understands Your Day**
Echo, your personal AI companion, reads your day and tells you something meaningful about it. Not data. Not metrics. A warm, observational sentence that makes you feel seen: "You spent a long afternoon at the library today — the third day in a row."

**Smart Pattern Recognition**
Over time, Unfold notices patterns in your life and gently surfaces them. It's like having a thoughtful friend who pays attention: "You've been going to that coffee shop every Tuesday."

**Ask About Your Life**
Chat with Echo to explore your own data. "How many times did I exercise this week?" "Where did I spend most of my time last month?" Your life data, accessible through natural conversation.

**Privacy First**
All data stays on your device. Location coordinates never leave your phone. AI features send only anonymized activity summaries — place names and durations, never GPS coordinates.

**Zero Effort**
No buttons to press. No check-ins. No streaks. Just carry your phone and live your life. Unfold does the rest.
