Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026
Unfold is designed with a local-first philosophy. Your location, activity, and personal data stay on your device by default. We do not track you for advertising, and we do not upload your raw location coordinates to any server.

1. What Data Unfold Uses

With your permission, Unfold may access the following data to generate your daily life timeline:

  • Location data: Places you visit and how long you stay, recorded via background location services. Used to build your daily timeline.
  • Motion & activity data: Walking, running, cycling, and driving activity detected by device sensors. Used to infer events like commutes and exercise.
  • Your manual inputs: Mood records, text notes, photos, and annotations you choose to add.

2. AI Features & Data Processing

Unfold's AI features (Echo daily summary, pattern insights, and Echo conversation) process your data as follows:

  • What is sent to AI: Anonymized activity summaries only — place names (e.g., "home", "library"), activity types (e.g., "walking 30 min"), and durations. These are derived from your raw sensor data on-device.
  • What is NOT sent: Raw GPS coordinates, precise location data, personal identity information, photos, or any data that could pinpoint your exact location.
  • AI provider: DeepSeek, accessed via API. Your activity summaries are processed to generate natural language insights.
  • Data retention: AI-generated summaries are stored locally on your device. The AI provider processes your data transiently and does not retain it.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. There is no cloud sync, no server-side database, and no account system. Your data exists only on your iPhone.

4. Background Location

Unfold uses "Always" location permission to passively record your location throughout the day, even when the app is closed. This is the core functionality of the app — without it, the daily timeline cannot be generated. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Your Control

  • Revoke location or motion permissions anytime in iOS Settings.
  • Clear all local data (annotations, mood records, AI summaries) from Unfold's Settings page.
  • Delete the app to remove all data permanently.

6. Children

Unfold is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
